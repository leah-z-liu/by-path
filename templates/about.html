    About getting a safer path

    Behind the scene, we're comparing 4 more paths alongside the original route to try and find the one with the least amount of violent crimes. This doesn't always work because there's no guarantee that the new paths we add are indeed safer. We're working on optimizing this feature. Stay tune!
